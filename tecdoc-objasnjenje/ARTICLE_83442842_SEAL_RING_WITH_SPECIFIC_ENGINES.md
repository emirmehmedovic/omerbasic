### Query 1: Pronaƒëi sve automobile sa specifiƒçnim motorima

```sql
SELECT DISTINCT
  m.Description as 'Manufacturer',
  mo.Description as 'Model',
  pc.Description as 'Motor',
  e.Description as 'Engine_Code',
  COUNT(DISTINCT pc.id) as 'Variants',
  MIN(YEAR(pc.From)) as 'From_Year',
  MAX(YEAR(pc.To)) as 'To_Year'
FROM article_oe_numbers aon
LEFT JOIN passengercars pc ON 1=1
LEFT JOIN passengercars_link_engines ple ON ple.car_id = pc.id
LEFT JOIN engines e ON e.id = ple.engine_id
LEFT JOIN models mo ON mo.id = pc.Model
LEFT JOIN manufacturers m ON m.id = pc.ManufacturerId
WHERE aon.article_id = 83442842
  AND m.id = aon.Manufacturer
GROUP BY m.Description, mo.Description, pc.Description, e.Description
ORDER BY m.Description, mo.Description, pc.Description
LIMIT 500;
```
